Songs of Achillies

Madeline Miller is the author of Songs of Achillies, a novel that is set during the Greek Heroic Age. It is a retelling of the Trojan War as told from the perspective of Patroclus.

//book2//

Pride and Prejudice

Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen is a novel of manners. It follows the character development of Elizabeth Bennet, the protagonist of the book, who learns about the repercussions of hasty judgments and comes to appreciate the difference between superficial goodness and actual goodness.

//book3//

The Alchemist

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho inspires readers to follow their dreams and trust the journey of life.

//book4//

Ikigai the Japanese secret

Ikigai the Japanese secretBook by Francesc Miralles and Hector Garcia. A short non-fiction that takes a close look at Japan and how people live as long as they do there. It is a treasure trove of knowledge.
